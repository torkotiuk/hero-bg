SVG image

  1. Find icon on f.e. icomoon.io
  2. Press "Get code" under icon
  3. Copy "Symbol definition(s)" from <symbol> to <symbol> to your sprite.svg
  4. Put the next line to your html:
    • <svg> width="24" height="16" aria-label="Facebook icon"> <use class="icon-menu" href="./images/sprite.svg#icon-menu"></use> </svg>
    - where #icon-menu is id from sprite.svg
  5. Style this svg according to class="icon-menu"

Additional info:

  • SVG values - stroke, width, linecap & dasharray
  • If we get svg-s from f.e. Adobe Ilustrator we should optimize svg with service svgomg --- ( SVGOMG service explanation on webinar )
