Tag picture allows to download a pic in different formats.

Browsers automatically download webp formats picture.

PICTURE guide (first technique):

  • Make every size (320px, 480px, etc.) of pictures to webp format by squoosh.app
  • Make markup like
    <picture>
    <source
    srcset="
    ./assets/images/cat-320.webp 320w,
    ./assets/images/cat-640.webp 640w,
    ./assets/images/cat-960.webp 960w
    "
    type="image/webp"
    />
    <img
    src="./assets/images/cat-320.jpg"
    alt="Cat"
    srcset="
    ./assets/images/cat-320.jpg 320w,
    ./assets/images/cat-640.jpg 640w,
    ./assets/images/cat-960.jpg 960w
    "
    width="400"
    />
    </picture>

Picture guide (second technique):

Different images for different screens

  • Download pics from template (in our example cat-portrait.jpg - for mobile, cat-landscape-normal.jpg - for tablet, cat-landscape-wide.jpg - for desktop)
